My Manggo

Let's make it cool!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups chopped fresh mango
  • ¾ cup cold milk
  • ¼ cup vanilla yogurt
  • 3 ice cubes or to taste
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Blend mango, milk, yogurt, ice, and vanilla extract in a blender until smooth and creamy.
  3. Give your love sincerely
  4. My mango is ready
